Ramen

Ingredients

  • Store bought pack of ramen.
  • 2 cups of water
  • Pack of flavoring

Steps

  1. Put water into a sauce pan
  2. Bring the water to a boil
  3. Insert the ramen
  4. Get a fork and push the ramen into the water with the fork
  5. Stir the ramen around untill it's nolonger a block

Stirfryed rice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of instant white rice (for convinence)
  • 1 cup of water(as said on the box)
  • 1-2 eggs (I reccomend 1 egg but it depends on the amount of rice)
  • 1 chicken breast(not needed)
  • frozen vegtables(not needed)

Steps

  1. Put 1 cup of water into a pot of some sort
  2. Bring the water to a boil
  3. Stir the water as you add the rice
  4. Put a lid on the pot
  5. wait 5 minutes
  6. while you're waiting for 5 minutes prepare the vegtables,egg, and chicken
  7. Put frozen vegtables in a bowl until it has reached your desired amount
  8. microwave the vegtables for 3-4 minutes (depending on quantity)
  9. Cook the chiken in a sauce pan untill it has reached a internal temp of 165
  10. After cooking the chicken cut it into small chunks
  11. put everything into a large sauce pan and cook it untill everything is hot
  12. put some sort of sauce into it untill it tastes good to you
  13. and enjoy :)
